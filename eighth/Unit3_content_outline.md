## Unit 3 learning outcomes (after S16–S25)
1. Describe **digital markets** — their characteristics, components, and types.
2. Distinguish **competition, cooperation, and co-opetition**, and explain why digital markets reward cooperation.
3. Explain the **layered internet model** (infrastructure/platform/application) and why layer control confers power.
4. Define **digital innovation**, its types and enablers, and distinguish innovation from invention.
5. Compare **digital business models** (the 10 types) and **value-creation models** (incl. value chain/shop/network).
6. Explain how **digital markets are modeled** — pricing, dynamic pricing, two-sided markets, tipping.
7. Explain digital **strategy** — value capture, ecosystem strategy, control points — and integrate all dimensions.

**S18 — Cooperation in Digital Markets.** Concepts: (1) what cooperation is + why digital markets encourage it (modularity, APIs, network effects, convenience); (2) 5 reasons to cooperate ("grow the pie first"); (3) 3 forms — joint platforms (NCHL, ConnectIPS, SWIFT), partnerships (co-branding/logistics/marketing), service integration. Tables: why-cooperate cause→effect; 3 forms of cooperation × Nepal example; cooperation benefits. Image: `s18_cooperation.png` (optional)/reuse.

**S19 — Co-opetition.** Concepts: (1) co-opetition = cooperate upstream (tech/infra) + compete downstream (customers/brand) + Value-Net idea (add fresh); (2) why common (layered products, ecosystems win); (3) benefits & risks (dependency, power imbalance, lock-in). Tables: cooperate-upstream vs compete-downstream (examples); co-opetition in Nepali business (eSewa+Nabil, Daraz+Pathao, banks+ConnectIPS); benefits vs risks. Image: `s19_coopetition.png`.

**S20 — The Layered Internet Model.** Concepts: (1) the 3 layers (infrastructure/platform/application) + user; (2) value flows bottom-up (technical→capability→experience); (3) power: control of lower layers → power over higher (Nepal dependence). Tables: the 3 layers × role × global vs Nepal example; value type by layer; benefits of layered architecture. Image: `s20_layers.png`.

**S21 — Digital Innovation.** Concepts: (1) definition + innovation ≠ invention + sustaining vs **disruptive** (add fresh); (2) the 6 types (product/process/business-model/service/marketing/organizational); (3) enablers (AI/IoT/cloud/big data/AR/VR), process (ideation→MVP→scale) & Nepal challenges.
